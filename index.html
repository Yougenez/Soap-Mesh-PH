About Us

Prior to the onset of the global COVID-19 pandemic, a small soap mesh brand from the Philippines, Soap Mesh PH, was quietly coming into the limelight. Founded in December 2019, the brand was launched on February 28, 2020, right around the time when every business across the globe was bound to face turmoil. Even with the pandemic’s economic turmoil, supply chain issues, and stock shortages, Soap Mesh PH was still able to sustain and grow thanks to the adaptive strategies in place that emphasized customer feedback. Soap Mesh PH is best known for their flagship product, the soap net, which is an exceptional innovation in bath accessories that completely changes the user’s bathing experience. Sold out in a matter of days after its initial release, the soap net combines a scrub and a soap holder to help produce a rich, creamy lather from bar soaps while preserving the user's soap’s quality, fragrance, and structure. The practical benefits are extraordinary for consumers looking to diversify. While the soap net offers everything regarding skincare, it does serve a secondary purpose as a storage organizational tool. It allows users to hang the soap net in the shower or near sink area. This means soap dishes do not need to be used, which frequently encumber limited bathroom counter space. This practical storage solution from Soap Mesh PH enhances decluttered hygiene spaces even for those with tidy bathrooms. A vital element distinguishing Soap Mesh PH from its competitors is the collaboration with Gene-Zero, a toy store which shares Soap Mesh’s dedication to quality and creativity. This partnership demonstrates the forward-thinking marketing Soap Mesh PH applies to personal care products. Even if the soap net remains a functional product, the addition of excitement and creativity, possibly through design or packaging, appeals to younger audiences who appreciate aesthetic and novelty. Soap Mesh PH’s journey showcases a startup’s transformation into a well-known brand through the application of unyielding perseverance, adaptability, strategic customer engagement, and brand-building over time. The company experienced stunted growth due to substantial and impactful challenges in the beginning; rather than retreating, they innovated. Through promising constant optimization and effort towards customer satisfaction, these strategies developed a loyal following, coupled with meaningful partnerships which began to add value and creativity to the brand’s offerings. For instance, in its final remarks, Soap Mesh PH serves as a proof of the effects of modern innovation and customer-centric growth strategies. The brand has established a mark in the personal care industry because of its high quality, sustainable, and meticulously crafted products. Enhanced functionality and user experience means that Soap Mesh PH doesn’t just foster good skincare routines, but also convenience and creativity in everyday life.

Logo Description

Soap Mesh PH originated as a practical solution to reduce soap waste and enhance lather during bathing. Initially popularized in Japan and other parts of Asia, it was designed to make bar soap more efficient, hygienic, and exfoliating. Over time, it gained global use for its convenience and effectiveness in daily skincare routines.
